Exam Day Protocol
⏱️
Time split
Exam Strategy
  • →1-mark Qs first (≈60 min), then 2-mark Qs.
  • →Never spend more than 4 min on a single question.
  • →If stuck, Mark for Review and move on immediately.
  • →Time management is crucial.
🚫
MCQ negative marking
Penalty Rules
  • →−⅓ for 1-mark MCQ, −⅔ for 2-mark MCQ.
  • →Skip if unsure. Guessing blindly will destroy your rank!
  • →MSQ and NAT: always attempt, no penalty.
  • →Be smart.
🎯
Score target breakdown
The 75+ Goal
  • →GA: 13+/15
  • →Engg Maths: 10+/13
  • →Core: 57+/72. Don't sacrifice GA for core.
  • →Prioritize accuracy over attempt rate.
🧠
What AIR <100 looks like
Reality Check
  • →Score ≥75.
  • →That means getting ~85% of GA+Maths right.
  • →And ~70% of core. Perfectly achievable.
  • →Don't let a bad question ruin your rhythm.
📝
MSQ strategy
Multiple Select
  • →All selected options must be correct for marks.
  • →Partial credit = 0. Don't guess on MSQ.
  • →Read every single option twice.
  • →MSQs are designed to trick you.
🔢
NAT strategy
Numerical Answer
  • →No options, no negative. Always attempt.
  • →Answers within 1% tolerance usually accepted.
  • →Double-check with virtual calculator.
